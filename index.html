You're ready to buy a home when you have stable income, a good credit score (typically 620+), a down payment saved (usually 10-20% of the home price), and you plan to stay in the area for at least 3-5 years. Our team can help assess your financial readiness and connect you with mortgage pre-approval services.

Spring and early summer (March-June) are typically the best times to sell, as more buyers are actively searching. However, market conditions vary by location, and our agents can provide specific insights for your area.

The offer amount depends on comparable home sales, market conditions, property condition, and your budget. Our experienced agents will help you craft a competitive offer that protects your interests.

Closing costs typically range from 2-5% of the home price and include fees for appraisal, title insurance, and loan processing. Buyers and sellers each have specific costs they're responsible for, and some costs can be negotiated.
